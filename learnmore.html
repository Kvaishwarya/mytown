Planning A Trip To Udupi? Make Sure To Include These Must-Do Activities

Please choose a filter option to view the content:

Udupi Sri Krishna Temple

The Udupi Sri Krishna Temple is the heart of Udupi

Address: Temple Car St, Sri Krishna Temple Complex, Thenkpete, Maruthi Veethika, Udupi, Karnataka Timings: 4:30 AM – 9:30 PM


Kadiyali Sri Mahishamardini Temple

It is considered as one of the oldest temples in Udupi. It was built during the 7th century and is considered as an old form of Shri Krishna Temple.

Address: Kadiyali Temple Rd, Kunjibettu, Udupi, Karnataka Timings: 8:00 AM and 8:00 PM


Vadabhandeshwara Temple

At a distance of 10 Kms from Udupi Railway Station, Vadabhandeshwara Temple dedicated to Balarama, the brother of Sri Krishna is situated near Malpe Beach. This temple is believed to have been built by Madhvacharya who built the famous Udupi Sri Krishna Temple.

Address: Vadabandeshwara Fruits And Vegetables Shop Vadabhandeshwara, Near Balaram Temple, Malpe, Karnataka 576108, India, Malpe. Timings: 6 AM - 8 PM


Siddhi Vinayaka Temple Anegudde

At a distance of 25 km from Udupi, 86 km from Mangalore & 70 km from Murudeswar, Anegudde Vinayaka Temple is a Hindu temple located in Kumbhashi between Udupi & Karwar.

Milagres Church

The Our Lady of the Miracles Cathedral, also known as Milagres Cathedral was originally established in the year 1680. Later in the year 1881, a treaty was signed between Rani Chennamma of Bednur and the Portuguese which resulted in the redevelopment of this beautiful church. Address: Santhekatte, Udupi, Karnataka 576114 Mass Timings: Monday to Saturday: 6:30 AM and Sunday: 6:30 AM, 8:00 AM, and 9:30 AM.

Holy Rosary Church

Holy Rosary Church in Udupi was established in 1682. This beautiful church is located on the western side of the city amidst the panoramic landscapes exhibiting delightful vistas for nature lovers. Address: The Parish Priest, Holy Rosary Church, Church Road, Kundapura – 576 201, Karnataka, India

Christ Church

The Mother of Sorrows Parish is an amazing chapel which was established in 1880. The Bishop of Mangalore in 1880 commissioned an establishment of the independent Udupi Parish and appointed Father J.N. Masse as the first Parish priest here. Address: Mother of Sorrows Church, K. M. Marge, Church Road, Udupi – 576 101

Mother Of Sorrows Church

The Christ Church was established in 1967 and was inaugurated by late Cardinal Valerian Gracias. The Christ Church had been the center of Youth with numerous young believers visiting the place for regular prayers Address: Parish Priest, Christ Church, Alevoor Road, Manipal Post, 576104, Udupi Dist. Karnataka Mass Timings: Monday to Saturday: 6:30 AM & Sunday: 8:00 AM and 5:30 PM
